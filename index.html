About US

We are a personal yoga studio with a large selection. Here yoga people of all ages and our aim is to share all parts of the yoga system. Here you can sweat in a dynamic asana practice, or meet silence in meditation. Our open schedule provides access to about twenty classes with some of Stockholm's foremost teachers. If you want to immerse yourself, you can take a course or workshop.
